GLIDER

So what if you've run out of fuel and death is imminent? Stay in the air as long as you can!

How to play:

1) Move left, right, or down with the arrow keys. You can't move up!
2) Avoid obstacles. Crashing into them will lower your elevation.
3) Fly into wind drafts. They will increase your elevation.
4) The game ends when you land on the ground.

Press SPACEBAR to play!
